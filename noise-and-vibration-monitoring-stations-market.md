<p><p>Noise and vibration monitoring stations can be classified into two main types: portable and fixed. In contrast, fixed monitoring stations are permanently installed at specific sites to continuously track noise and vibration levels over time, ensuring ongoing compliance and providing long-term data for environmental or regulatory purposes. Both types serve essential roles in noise and vibration management.</p></p>
