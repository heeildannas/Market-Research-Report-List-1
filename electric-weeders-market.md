<p><p>The electric weeders market is divided into two main types: corded and cordless. Corded electric weeders are powered through an electrical outlet, offering consistent power and unlimited runtime, making them suitable for larger areas. However, they may limit mobility due to the need for an extension cord. On the other hand, cordless electric weeders operate on rechargeable batteries, providing greater flexibility and ease of use in hard-to-reach spots, though they have a limited operating time before requiring a recharge.</p></p>
