<p><p>The Biopharma Track and Trace Solutions market primarily includes two types of technologies: barcodes and RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification). Barcodes utilize printed labels with unique scannable patterns, enabling efficient tracking of pharmaceutical products through supply chains. RFID, on the other hand, employs radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to products, offering enhanced data storage and retrieval capabilities. Both systems serve to ensure product authenticity, improve inventory management, and enhance regulatory compliance in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
