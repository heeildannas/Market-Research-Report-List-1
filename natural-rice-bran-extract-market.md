<p><strong>The Natural Rice Bran Extract Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction Extract</li><li>Squeezing Extract</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Natural Rice Bran Extract market is divided into two main types based on the extraction methods: Extraction Extract and Squeezing Extract. Extraction Extract involves using solvents to obtain beneficial compounds from rice bran, resulting in a concentrated product often rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and fatty acids. In contrast, Squeezing Extract utilizes mechanical pressure to extract oil and nutrients without solvents, preserving the natural integrity of the rice bran. Both methods cater to diverse applications in food, skincare, and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
