<p><p>Dental surgical ultrasonic generators are categorized into two main types based on their power source. Self-excited power sources generate ultrasonic waves internally, often resulting in compact and efficient devices suitable for various dental procedures. In contrast, external excited power sources rely on external systems to create ultrasonic vibrations, providing flexibility in application but may require additional equipment. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing precision and efficiency in dental surgeries, improving outcomes in patient care and treatment.</p></p>
