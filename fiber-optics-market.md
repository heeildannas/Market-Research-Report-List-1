<p><strong>The Fiber Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optics market is primarily divided into two types: Multi-Mode Fiber Optics and Single-Mode Fiber Optics. Multi-Mode Fiber Optics, characterized by a larger core diameter, allows multiple light modes to propagate, making it suitable for shorter distances and local area networks. In contrast, Single-Mode Fiber Optics features a smaller core that enables only one light mode, facilitating long-distance communication with higher bandwidth capacity. Each type serves distinct applications, driving demand across telecommunications, data centers, and enterprise networks.</p></p>
