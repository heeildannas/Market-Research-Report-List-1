<p><p>The medical nasal aspirator market features various types designed for effective mucus removal. Pump-type aspirators utilize manual or electrical pumps to create suction. Suction mouth types rely on the user's mouth to generate suction, making them portable and simple. Spray-type aspirators administer saline solutions to loosen mucus before collection, enhancing comfort. Steam-type aspirators use heated steam to help clear nasal passages through vaporization. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to patients with varying preferences and medical requirements.</p></p>
