<p><p>The Metro Turnstile market includes primarily two types: Optical Turnstiles and Arm Turnstiles. Optical Turnstiles utilize infrared sensors to detect passenger movement, allowing for seamless entry while minimizing fare evasion. They provide a modern, sleek design suitable for high-traffic areas. In contrast, Arm Turnstiles feature physical barriers that require users to swipe a card or ticket to gain access, providing a more controlled entry point. Both types enhance security and streamline passenger flow in transit systems.</p></p>
