<p><p>The Ultra High Speed Machine Vision Cameras market can be divided into two primary types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images in a single line, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous imaging, such as conveyor belt inspections. In contrast, area scan cameras capture full images at once, suitable for static or slower-moving objects. Both categories cater to diverse industrial needs, including quality control, automation, and robotics, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in various processes.</p></p>
