<p><ul><li>AC Piezoelectric Motors</li><li>DC Piezoelectric Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric motors market consists of two primary types: AC piezoelectric motors and DC piezoelectric motors. AC piezoelectric motors operate using alternating current to induce mechanical movement through piezoelectric materials, offering advantages in terms of smooth motion and efficiency. In contrast, DC piezoelectric motors use direct current, providing precise control and rapid response, making them suitable for applications requiring fine positioning and high torque. Both types are widely utilized in robotics, medical devices, and precision instruments.</p></p>
