<p><p>The Labels and Flexible Packaging Laser Die Cutting Machine market is segmented into two main types: Rotary Die Cutting and Semi-Rotary Die Cutting. Rotary Die Cutting involves the use of a rotating die to cut through materials continuously, making it ideal for high-volume production of label and flexible packaging. In contrast, Semi-Rotary Die Cutting combines both rotary and flatbed techniques, allowing for versatility in cutting various shapes and reducing waste. Both methods cater to diverse packaging applications, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
