<p><p>Data annotation and labeling tools are categorized into three market types based on the level of human intervention: </p><p>1. **Manual Data Annotation** involves human annotators tagging data, ensuring high accuracy but is time-consuming and costly. </p><p>2. **Semi-supervised Annotation** combines human input with algorithmic assistance, allowing for faster processes while maintaining reasonable accuracy. </p><p>3. **Automatic Annotation** uses advanced algorithms to label data independently, offering speed and scalability but may sacrifice some accuracy. Each type serves different needs depending on the project's requirements.</p></p>
