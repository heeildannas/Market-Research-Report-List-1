<p><p>Enterprise Document Management Systems (EDMS) are categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed locally on an organization's servers, offering greater control over security and data management but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions host documents on remote servers, providing accessibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. This model allows for easier collaboration and updates, making it suitable for organizations prioritizing flexibility and reduced IT overhead.</p></p>
