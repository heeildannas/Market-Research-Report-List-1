<p><p>The 3D and 4D military radar market is categorized by frequency bands, each serving distinct surveillance and tracking needs. UHF and VHF bands are used for long-range detection and communication. S-band is effective for weather tracking and air traffic control, while L-band offers better ground penetration. X-band provides high-resolution imaging, and C-band is utilized for medium-range applications. K, Ku, and Ka-bands offer higher frequencies for advanced targeting and missile guidance, enhancing situational awareness and operational effectiveness in military applications.</p></p>
