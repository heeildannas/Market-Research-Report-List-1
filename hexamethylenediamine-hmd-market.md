<p><strong>The Hexamethylenediamine (HMD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adiponitrile Method</li><li>Hexanediol Method</li><li>Caprolactam Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hexamethylenediamine (HMD) is produced primarily through three methods: the Adiponitrile Method, the Hexanediol Method, and the Caprolactam Method. The Adiponitrile Method involves the hydrogenation of adiponitrile, while the Hexanediol Method utilizes hexanediol as a precursor, both resulting in HMD suitable for nylon production. The Caprolactam Method converts caprolactam, typically used in producing nylon 6, into HMD. Each method offers distinct advantages regarding cost, yield, and environmental impact, catering to varying market demands.</p></p>
