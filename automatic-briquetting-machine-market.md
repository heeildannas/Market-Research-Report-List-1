<p><p>The Automatic Briquetting Machine market comprises three main types: Screw Type, Hydraulic Type, and Piston Type. Screw Type machines use a rotating screw to compress material into briquettes, ensuring uniform density. Hydraulic Type machines utilize hydraulic pressure for efficient compression, ideal for high-density briquettes. Piston Type machines employ a piston mechanism, providing high pressure for forming briquettes from various raw materials. Each type caters to specific operational needs, varying in production capacity, efficiency, and the types of materials processed.</p></p>
