<p><p>The grouting material market primarily includes urethane-based and epoxy-based grouts. Urethane-based grouts are flexible, water-resistant, and ideal for applications requiring elasticity, such as in foundations and concrete repairs. They offer excellent adhesion and durability. Epoxy-based grouts, on the other hand, provide high strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making them suitable for industrial and structural applications. Both types cater to distinct needs, influencing their market demand based on performance requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
