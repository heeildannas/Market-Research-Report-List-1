<p><strong>The Impervious Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Impervious Concrete</li><li>Admixture Impervious Concrete</li><li>Expansive Impervious Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impervious concrete market comprises three main types: Ordinary Impervious Concrete, which is designed to prevent water penetration through its dense composition; Admixture Impervious Concrete, enhanced with specific additives that improve permeability resistance; and Expansive Impervious Concrete, which incorporates materials that expand upon curing to further seal pores and enhance impermeability. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to various construction needs, from water-retaining structures to surfaces requiring low water absorption and durability in harsh environments.</p></p>
