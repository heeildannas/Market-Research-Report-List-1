<p><p>The semiconductor molding compounds market comprises two primary types: liquid and granular. Liquid molding compounds are typically used in semiconductor encapsulation processes, allowing for precise filling of intricate geometries and providing excellent thermal and electrical insulation. Granular molding compounds, on the other hand, are pre-formed materials that are heated and molded into desired shapes during production. Both types play crucial roles in protecting semiconductor devices, enhancing their performance, and ensuring reliability in various electronic applications.</p></p>
