<p><p>Borescope inspection cameras are classified into two main types: cordless and corded. Cordless borescopes offer flexibility and mobility, allowing users to access tight or hard-to-reach spaces without being tethered to a power source. They typically rely on battery power, making them ideal for remote inspections. In contrast, corded borescopes provide continuous power and often feature higher-resolution imaging capabilities, making them suitable for industrial applications where consistent performance and longer usage times are essential. Both types serve distinct needs in various inspection scenarios.</p></p>
