<p><p>The infrared windows market is categorized into two primary types: crystal materials and polymer materials. Conversely, polymer materials, like polyimide and fluoropolymer, offer flexibility, lightweight characteristics, and cost-effectiveness, catering to applications requiring ease of installation and usage. Both material types serve critical roles in various industries, including defense, telecommunications, and industrial sensing, meeting diverse operational needs.</p></p>
