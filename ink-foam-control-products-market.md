<p><p>The Ink Foam Control Products market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based categories. Water-based foam control agents utilize water as the primary solvent, making them less harmful to the environment and safer for handling. They effectively reduce foam in water-based inks. On the other hand, solvent-based products are formulated with organic solvents, providing robust foam control in solvent-based ink systems. These products cater to specific industry needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and user safety considerations.</p></p>
