<p><strong>The Flow-through Cell Dissolution Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV On</li><li>UV Offline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow-through cell dissolution tester market is segmented into two main types: UV On and UV Offline systems. UV On systems continuously monitor the dissolution process in real time using ultraviolet spectroscopy, providing immediate data on active pharmaceutical ingredient release. In contrast, UV Offline systems collect samples during dissolution and analyze them separately, allowing for batch analysis. Each type serves specific laboratory and regulatory needs, offering options for real-time monitoring or post-dissolution evaluation in pharmaceutical quality control.</p></p>
