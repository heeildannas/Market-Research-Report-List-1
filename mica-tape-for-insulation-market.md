<p><ul><li>Mica Glass Tape</li><li>Mica Polyester Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mica tape for insulation primarily comprises two main types: mica glass tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass tape features a blend of mica and fiberglass, offering exceptional thermal stability and electrical insulation, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, mica polyester tape incorporates a polyester film with mica, providing flexibility and improved moisture resistance, ideal for a range of electrical insulation needs. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for their reliable performance.</p></p>
