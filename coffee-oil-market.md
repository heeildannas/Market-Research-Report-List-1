<p><strong>The Coffee Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Pressed Oils</li><li>CO2/Solvent Extraction Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee oil market can be categorized into two primary types: cold pressed oils and CO2/solvent extraction oils. Cold pressed oils are obtained by mechanically pressing coffee beans without heat, preserving their natural flavor and aroma. In contrast, CO2 and solvent extraction oils involve using carbon dioxide or chemical solvents to efficiently extract oil, often resulting in higher yields but potentially altering flavor profiles. Each method caters to different consumer preferences and applications, influencing the market dynamics of coffee oils.</p></p>
