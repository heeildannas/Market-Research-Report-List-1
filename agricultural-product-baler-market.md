<p><p>The agricultural product baler market primarily consists of two types: round balers and square balers. Square balers, on the other hand, generate rectangular bales, allowing for more efficient storage and stacking. Each type caters to different farming needs, influencing purchasing decisions based on crop type, storage capacity, and transportation requirements.</p></p>
