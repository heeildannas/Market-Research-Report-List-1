<p><strong>The PTFE Micropowder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monomer Polymerization</li><li>Resin Degradation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE micropowder market is categorized into two main types based on production processes: monomer polymerization and resin degradation. Monomer polymerization involves synthesizing polytetrafluoroethylene from monomers, resulting in fine powders with uniform particle sizes, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, resin degradation involves breaking down existing PTFE resins into micropowder form, allowing for cost-effective production. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including coatings, lubricants, and composite materials, offering unique properties suited to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
