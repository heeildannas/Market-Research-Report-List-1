<p><strong>The Home Use ECG Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG Monitor</li><li>Holter ECG Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home use ECG monitor market includes two main types: Resting ECG Monitors and Holter ECG Monitors. Resting ECG Monitors are designed for short-term monitoring, providing users with immediate data about their heart's electrical activity during a stationary state. In contrast, Holter ECG Monitors are portable devices that record heart activity over extended periods, typically 24 to 48 hours, allowing for the detection of irregularities that may not appear during a brief assessment, enhancing long-term heart health monitoring at home.</p></p>
