<p><ul><li>Direct Gas Supplement</li><li>Indirect Gas Supplement</li><li>Non-gas Supplement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Make-up Air Units (MAUs) are essential for maintaining indoor air quality and managing building pressure. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Direct Gas Supplement**: These units utilize gas-fired heating to directly warm incoming air for immediate use.</p><p>2. **Indirect Gas Supplement**: In this type, gas heat transfers through a heat exchanger, avoiding direct contact with the air, offering more control and combustion safety.</p><p>3. **Non-gas Supplement**: These systems rely on electric or other forms of heating, suitable for areas where gas is unavailable or not preferred.</p></p>
