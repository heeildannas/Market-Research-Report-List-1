<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spark plug market can be categorized into hot and cold spark plugs based on their heat range and application. Hot spark plugs operate at higher temperatures, preventing fouling in engines with low combustion heat, making them suitable for everyday driving. Cold spark plugs, conversely, dissipate heat more quickly, ideal for high-performance or racing engines that generate greater heat due to high-speed operation. Understanding these types helps vehicle owners and manufacturers choose the right spark plugs for optimal engine performance.</p></p>
