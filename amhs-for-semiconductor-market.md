<p><p>Automated Material Handling Systems (AMHS) are critical for semiconductor manufacturing, optimizing the movement of materials and enhancing efficiency. The market types include:</p><p>1. **STK (Storage and Retrieval Systems)**: Automated systems for storing and retrieving wafers.</p><p>2. **OHT (Overhead Transport)**: Systems that move materials overhead within fabrication facilities.</p><p>3. **OHS (Overhead Hoist Systems)**: Mechanisms for elevating and transporting materials.</p><p>4. **RGV (Rail Guided Vehicles)**: Automated guided vehicles that operate on tracks.</p><p>5. **AGV (Automated Guided Vehicles)**: Flexible vehicles navigating autonomously to transport materials.</p></p>
