<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline Solar Panel</li><li>Polycrystalline Solar Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household solar panel market primarily features two types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline panels. Monocrystalline panels are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and a sleek design, making them ideal for space-constrained homes. They tend to be more expensive. In contrast, polycrystalline panels are manufactured from multiple silicon crystals, making them more affordable but slightly less efficient. Both types serve to harness solar energy for residential use, catering to different budgets and energy needs in sustainable living.</p></p>
