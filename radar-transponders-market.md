<p><p>The radar transponders market can be categorized into C Band and X Band segments, each serving specific applications. C Band transponders operate in the 4 to 8 GHz frequency range, typically used for medium-range radar systems, including air traffic control and weather monitoring. In contrast, X Band transponders, operating in the 8 to 12 GHz range, are favored for high-resolution imaging and navigation systems, often utilized in military, marine, and environmental monitoring applications. Each type caters to distinct operational requirements and performance attributes.</p></p>
